StillShelf is an Android audiobook player built for Audiobookshelf.

It is designed for people who want a clean, practical way to browse their library, manage books and bookmarks, and keep listening without fighting the interface.

Features include:
- Browse books, authors, narrators, series, collections, genres, bookmarks, playlists, and downloads
- Continue listening, jump back into finished books, and use a full-screen player built for long-form listening
- Create, edit, and delete bookmarks
- Download books for offline playback
- Support for Audiobookshelf libraries with m4b and mp3 audiobooks

StillShelf is made for self-hosted Audiobookshelf users, including setups on local networks or personal servers.
