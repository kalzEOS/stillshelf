# StillShelf Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 6, 2026

StillShelf is built to be safe and private by default.

## Short Version

- We do not collect your personal data.
- We do not sell your data.
- We do not run ads, trackers, analytics, or third-party profiling SDKs.
- Your data stays on your device and on the Audiobookshelf server(s) you choose.

## What StillShelf Stores

StillShelf stores app data locally on your device so the app can work, such as:

- app preferences (theme, layout, player settings),
- server entries you add,
- local session/auth data needed to stay signed in,
- local playback state and download metadata.

## What StillShelf Sends Over the Network

StillShelf only sends network requests to:

- Audiobookshelf server(s) you explicitly add, and
- GitHub endpoints used for optional update checks and release notes.

We do not send your data to us (the StillShelf developers).

## No Developer Data Collection

StillShelf developers do not collect, buy, share, rent, or sell your personal data.

## Your Control

You can control your data at any time:

- sign out of servers,
- remove saved server entries,
- delete downloads,
- clear app data from your device settings.
